Rajinikanth after ‘Annaatthe’ signed up for his next film with Nelson Dilipkumar. Titled ‘Jailer’ the movie stars Rajinikanth in the lead role alongside Mohanlal. Jacki Shroff, Ramya Krishnan, Tamannaah Bhatia, Shiva Rajkumar, Sunil, Yogi Babu and Vasanth Ravi in important roles. The music for the movie is composed by Anirudh Ravichander.
